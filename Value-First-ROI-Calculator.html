Value-First ROI Calculator

What this is: A fast way to anchor price increase discussions in ROI—so you can move from defensiveness to confidence.

How to use it: Enter the current contract value, the total monetized value you've delivered, and your proposed increase. The calculator shows:

  • Your current ROI multiple (value ÷ contract)
  • What ROI would be after a price increase
  • New contract value after increase

Ideal for CS teams prepping for renewal, pricing, or upsell conversations with finance or procurement stakeholders.
